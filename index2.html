Welcome!

  • Welcome to my blog! This is a good old-fashioned hand-crafted website. No flashy graphics, no SEO, analytics or ads, and no pre-made templates. Just me, some code I learned when I was 12, and whatever thoughts decide to populate my brain. Enjoy!

  • I started this website in April 2023, and I chose to handwrite it because with the opening-of-access to AI and the innovative ways we come up with to use it, "perfect" content is always achievable now on with no more than the slightest of effort. Why write uniquely when you can have the AI write "correctly"? I'd like to have my corner of the internet be handmade in a way that shows, including in the imperfections. I proofread, but generally won't use a spellchecker; if I miss it I miss it. Kintsugi in website form.
